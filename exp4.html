Fun Science Facts

Honey never spoils!

Archaeologists even found edible honey in ancient Egyptian tombs!

Water can boil and freeze at the same time.

This phenomenon is called the triple point and occurs under special conditions.

Octopuses have three hearts.

Two pump blood to the gills, and one pumps it to the rest of the body.
